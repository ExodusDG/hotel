Feed your imagination

Our grocers, chefs, baristas and mixologists work closely together and use the same freshly and responsibly sourced ingredients throughout the hotel. This ensures that wherever you are in The Gantry you can enjoy consistently excellent products, from the ground level, to room service, to the restaurant and terraces on the 18th floor.

Learn more

Hermanos

Founded by Victor and Santiago Gamboa, the two brothers are excited to be bringing their passion for speciality coffee to East London and the pop-up will be a celebration of Colombian culture and high-quality beans.

Learn more

Union Social

Our comfy all-day lounge, cocktail bar and relaxed chef's counter offer up a tempting selection of food and drink to keep you satisfied from early morning to late at night. Get some work done in the lounge, have lunch with colleagues on the terrace, cocktails with friends at the bar or just quietly disappear into a good book.

Learn more

Coupe

This sparkling bar is stocked with an impressive range of UK-sourced sparkling wines, refreshing sparkling teas, Kombucha, seltzers, spritzers and just about anything else that sparkles. Join us on the intimate terrace or find a snug spot inside where you can sit back (and sip back) in style.

Learn more

Sky Bar and Terraces

Our stunning rooftop restaurant, bar and terraces on the 18th floor offers one of the most spectacular panoramic views over London. Join us for a cocktail as the sun slips behind the capital’s skyline, then stay on for a delicious dinner followed by late-night entertainment. This is urban living at its finest.

Learn more

Meet the Neighbourhood

A creative community and world-class cultural destination. Explore a thriving food and drink scene, where park-side pop-ups rub shoulders with the best in modern fine dining. Track down something special at off-beat boutiques and international brands. Or dive into the best of the arts – from street art and galleries, to film and music.
